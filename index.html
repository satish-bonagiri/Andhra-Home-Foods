Our Story

Handcrafted in our kitchen, straight to your heart

Sri Godha Shabari Andhra Home Foods is a home-based food venture nestled in the heart of Akshayanagar, Bengaluru. We bring the rich, authentic flavors of Andhra Pradesh directly to your doorstep.

Every sweet, pickle and snack is prepared fresh using traditional family recipes — no artificial preservatives, no compromise on taste. We believe that the best food comes from a home kitchen filled with love and tradition.

We also supply authentic Andhra sweets and snacks for weddings, functions, corporate events and bulk catering. Place your order in advance and we'll make it fresh, just for you.
